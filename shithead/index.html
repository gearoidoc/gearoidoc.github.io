Rules

Basic Play

Play a card equal to or higher than the top of the pile. Can't play? Pick up the pile.

Special Cards

  • 2 Play on anything. Anything follows.
  • 7 Play on 7 or lower. Next player must play 7 or lower, or a special card.
  • 8 Play on anything. Two 8s = play again.
  • 10 Play on anything. Burns the pile, play again.

4 of a Kind

Four of the same rank on the pile burns it. Play again.

Card Phases

Play from your hand first, then face-up, then face-down (blind).
